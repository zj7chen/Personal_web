About me

Je suis tellement heureuse de le partager avec vous.

Howdy. My name is Jerry. I'm a sophomore of honours mathematics at the University of Waterloo. I write code, music and spend a lot of my spare time playing trombone. I work on lip trills, practice a couple pieces of songs and take a break to write my app. It almost became part of my daily routine. I won't deny the fact that I'm a self-motivated hard worker, but when I stay at home, I do try to relax as much as time allows. Currently I'm excited to take part in all kinds of Hackathons since they fulfill me with great pleasure and senses of pride and accomplishment.

Different experience

Determines the expression of intelligence.

I enjoy dabbling in many kinds of fields and careers to keep my life away from being boring and dreadful. I have over six years of practical hands-on experience in terms of coding, industrial design as well as dealing with hardware/software systems such as Linux, Raspberry Pi(LED Matrix) and Arduino Uno. I was once part of the design, build & programming team for First Robotics since 2013. The languages that I am specializing in are C++, Java, Python, OpenGL and Scheme.

I love that feeling of interacting and the challenge that Robotics brings. Usually I'll come up with a blueprint for one particular component with the use of Solidworks. Then it will be brought over to the wood shop to become fully assembled. The final step would be to use Codeblocks to write and import a series of codes so that the bot is capable of completing specific tasks assigned by me and the rest of the crew.
